Privacy Policy for Meteor Strike

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Meteor Strike is published by StrikingGames. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the game, what the game stores locally on your device, and how third-party services such as Google AdMob and Google Play Games Services may handle data.

Meteor Strike does not require you to create an account with StrikingGames. The game can be played without signing in. Google Play Games sign-in is optional and is only offered when you choose to use the leaderboard.

1. Information We Store Locally on Your Device

Meteor Strike stores a small amount of gameplay and settings data locally on your device, such as:

  • Your best score
  • Your music, sound effects, and haptics preferences
  • Temporary ad and privacy-related state needed for the app to function correctly

This local data is used only to operate the game experience on your device. It is not sent to a StrikingGames server because Meteor Strike does not currently use its own game backend.

2. Advertising and Consent

Meteor Strike uses Google AdMob to display ads, including rewarded ads and banner ads. AdMob and related Google technologies may process information such as device identifiers, IP address, approximate location derived from IP, app interactions, diagnostics, and ad performance information.

The game also uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request or manage consent when required by applicable law or by Google's policies. A privacy or consent message is not shown to every player by default. It is shown only when required for the player, region, and ad configuration in effect at that time.

When available, you can access privacy choices inside the game through the in-app privacy entry point.

3. Optional Google Play Games Leaderboard

Meteor Strike offers an optional Google Play Games leaderboard on Android. If you choose to tap the leaderboard button and sign in to Google Play Games, Google may process information associated with your Play Games account and leaderboard activity, such as your player profile, player identifier, and submitted score.

If you never use the leaderboard, the game does not require Play Games sign-in to play.

4. Third-Party Services

Meteor Strike currently uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob for advertising
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) for ad consent and privacy messaging
  • Google Play Games Services for the optional leaderboard feature

Google's Privacy Policy is available here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. How We Use Information

Information processed through the game and its service providers may be used to:

  • Run core gameplay features and save local preferences
  • Show ads, including rewarded ads
  • Support consent and privacy controls where required
  • Submit and display leaderboard scores if you choose to use the leaderboard
  • Maintain service integrity, prevent fraud, and diagnose technical issues

6. Data Sharing

StrikingGames does not sell your personal information directly. However, data may be processed by third-party providers listed above as part of advertising, consent management, and optional leaderboard functionality.

7. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device generally remains until you clear app data or uninstall the app. Data processed by third-party providers is subject to their own retention practices and policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Meteor Strike is not intended to knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to a third-party service through the app in a way that should be addressed, please contact us and we will review the issue.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to use reputable service providers and standard platform protections. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and third-party services operate under their own security practices.

10. External Links

The app or this page may contain links to external sites operated by third parties. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those third-party sites.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the date at the top.
